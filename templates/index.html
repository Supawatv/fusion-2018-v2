Question

Junk food consumption is correleated with people from low socio-economic income. Does the prevalence of short-term loans (payday lenders per 100,000 people)increases the prevalence of McDonalds and thereby junk food consumption (McDonalds per 100,000 people)

Results and Data

Prevalence of short-term loans per capita does not increase prevalence of McDonalds per capita.
